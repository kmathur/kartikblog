Blog in the Age of AI

Blog in the age of AI

In the age of AI, where machines can generate text, images, and even code, blogging might seem almost redundant. Yet, I write because blogging allows me to capture my thoughts, reflect deeply, and share a uniquely human perspective that no AI can replicate. It’s a space to explore ideas, document learning, and connect with readers in a personal way—reminding me that even in a world of automation, human curiosity and creativity remain invaluable.
