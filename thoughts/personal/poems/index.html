About Poetry

Poetry, for me, is about capturing those fleeting moments of clarity - the observations that emerge during mundane routines like commuting, the insights that surface in quiet contemplation, and the emotions that need form and structure to be understood.

"Poetry is when an emotion has found its thought and the thought has found words." - Robert Frost
